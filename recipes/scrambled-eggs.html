Scrambled Eggs

The ultimate breakfast, and super meal prepable. This dish is versatile and can be made to match almost anyone's tastes (unless they don't like eggs that is)

  • Two eggs
  • 1/4th cup of milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  1. Crack two eggs into a bowl
  2. Pour the milk, salt, and pepper into the bowl
  3. Use a fork or whisk to stir mixture until eggs and ingredients are well blended. They should have a smooth and creamy texture
  4. Use some butter, or oil spray to lubricate the pan
  5. Once the pan is hot, pour in the eggs slowly, and stir to keep them from burning
  6. When eggs have reached your desired consistency, remove onto a plate
  7. Serve with toast.
