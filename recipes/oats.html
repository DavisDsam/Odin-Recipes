Overnight Oats

Description

This recipe is a favorite of mine. I like to make this for my breakfast mealprep. I work late and wake up early so I need something quick and simple that I can just grab and go in the mornings.

Ingredients

  • Steel cut oats
  • Milk
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Honey
  • Fruit

Steps

  1. Pour one half cup of steel cut oats into a mason jar
  2. Add two tablespoons of greek yogurt and honey to your preffered amount of sweetness
  3. Add your toppings into the jar
  4. Use a spoon to stir the indredients in the jar so you have good distribution of toppings, honey and yogurt
  5. Pour one cup of milk into the jar and close tightly
  6. Store in the fridge overnight or for 3 to 4 hours
  7. Enjoy your breakfast!
