Peanut Butter Jelly

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is an american staple. It smells like family days at the beach and tasts like a job well done after practice. I tend to forget about this legendary snack for about 8 months out of the year, but when I remember I eat them until I'm sick of them again.

  • Peanut butter
  • Grape jelly
  • Honey wheat bread (two slices)
  1. Spread a your desired amount of peanut butter on one piece of honeywheat bread
  2. Spread slightly less grape jelly on the second piece of bread.
  3. Put the piece of cread togther, with the peanut butter and jelly facing each other.
  4. Serve with a cold glass of milk or water
